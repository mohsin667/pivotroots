How do you apply for a Flipkart Shopping Loan on ?

Download the app

Download the app

Go to the play store or app store and download the mobile application here.

Complete your profile

Upload documents and complete KYC & other required details

Click on “Shop” icon

Apply for “Flipkart Shopping Loan”

Select Flipkart from the multiple merchants featured on this screen.

Shopping loan disbursed to Flipkart gift card

Once your loan is approved, the money will be credited as Flipkart gift cards which can be redeemed to shop for any products on Flipkart.
